## Features

Use  <kbd>  Sprint  </kbd>  on water to swim faster!

<br>
<br>

## Mechanic

*While swimming faster you will use more energy.*

<br>

| Swim<br>Speed | Oxygen  | Hunger | Thirst
|:----------:|:-------:|:------:|:------:
| `> 1`      | `1.25x` | `1.2x` | `1.2x`
| `> 2`      | `1.5x`  | `1.3x` | `1.3x`
